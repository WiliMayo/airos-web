  <p>This challenge is based on the international <strong>F1TENTH</strong> platform: 1:10 scale autonomous vehicles designed and programmed to race on a track at top speed without human intervention.</p>

  <h3>Tech Stack and Implementation</h3>
  <p>This double podium is the result of the deep application of autonomous navigation algorithms in ROS/ROS2:</p>
  <ul style='text-align: left;'>
      <li><strong>Perception and Mapping:</strong> Use of SLAM algorithms to build precise track maps using LiDAR.</li>
      <li><strong>Localization:</strong> Implementation of Particle Filters to estimate the exact position on the track in real-time.</li>
      <li><strong>Path Planning:</strong> Optimization of Racelines to minimize lap times.</li>
      <li><strong>Control:</strong> Design of high-precision controllers, such as the <strong>Pure Pursuit (RPP)</strong> algorithm, for high-speed path tracking.</li>
  </ul>
