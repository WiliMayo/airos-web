About AIROS

More than a club, we are an innovation hub at ESPOL.

Our Mission

The Artificial Intelligence and Robotics Society (AIROS) was founded at ESPOL with a clear objective: to bridge the gap between academic theory and applied engineering.

We don't just build robots; we shape leaders capable of solving complex problems through cutting-edge algorithms, robust hardware, and teamwork. From international competitions to local workshops, we seek to democratize access to technology.

Our Vision

To become the national benchmark for student research in autonomous robotics and artificial intelligence, driving technological development in Ecuador.
